• Contributing towards fulfillment of Hon’ble PM’s Digital India initiative, Indian Railways decided to provide
  • Wi-Fi Broadband facility to Rail users at major Railway stations.
  • RailTel roped in Google as the technology partner for setting up fast Wi-Fi network initially
    covering 400 stations for providing fast Wi Fi to millions of passengers everyday.
  • Railwire Wi-Fi will be available to any user who has a working mobile connection on a smartphone.
  • The Wi-Fi service shall provide high speed connectivity for viewing uninterrupted high definition video,
    download of movies, songs, games by the passengers at Railway station
